Kokoda to Ower's Corner

Day 1 - Fly from Jackson's Airport to Girua Airport, Popondetta

Transport available to and from airport. Normal drive time is 3 hours and 30 minutes.

Overnight at Kokoda, dinner will be provided.

After dinner, the guides and porters will introduce themselves to the trekking group.


Day 2 - Kokoda Station to Isurava Memorial Site

5.00am - Rise and shine.

5.30am - 6.10am - Breakfast

6.20am - Trek briefing

6.30am - Start trekking for 5 hours before lunch with a break for 1 hour at Isurava Village.

1.30pm - Start trekking to Isurava Memorial Campsite, arriving at 3.00pm at the campsite.

3.00pm - Rest at campsite with an opportunity for a bath.

Sight seeing at the Isurava War Memorial site.

Dinner

Trek briefing for day 3

Bed time


Day 3 - Isurava Memorial Campsite to Abuari Village

6.00am - Rise and shine.

6.30am - Breakfast time.

7.30am - Trek briefing.

8.00am - Start trekking to Abuari Village.

Stop Ololi village, an old war operation site, for 10 minutes.

10.00am - Stop at Alola village for tea break, time from Isurava is approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Last break for waterfall photos at Ma-eaka waterfall, break for 30 minutes.

Arrive at Abuari village for overnight.

Vilage welcomes trekkers and porters.

Free time.

Tea and refreshment.

Dinner time.

Story telling.

Trek briefing for day 4.

Bed time.


Day 4 - Abuari village to Iora Creek Camp to Tempthons Cross 1 Campsite

5.30am - Rise and shine.

6.00am - Breakfast.

6.30am - Trek briefing.

7.00am - Start trekking to Iora Creek.

10.00am - Morning Tea at Iora Creek Campsite

Trek to Tempthons Cross to stay overnight at the campsite.

Opportunity for a swim and fishing at Iora River.

Set up tents at the camp site.

Dinner time.

Trek briefing for day 5.

Bed time.


Day 5 - Tempthons Cross 1 to Naduri village

5.30am - Rise and shine.

6.00am - Breakfast.

6.30am - Trek briefing.

7.00am - Start trekking to Naduri village.

Soft break at Kokoda look out.

Lunch break at 19 Hundred Campsite.

Sight seeing at the war site.

Arrive at Naduri village.

Set up tents.

Free time for bathing.

Dinner time.

Trek briefing for day 6.

Village Community Choir for entertainment.

Bed time.


Day 6 - Naduri Village to Manari Village

5.30am - Rise and shine.

6.00am - Breakfast time.

7.00am - Trek briefing.

7.30am - Start trekking to Manari village.

Lunch break and sight seeing.

Overnight at Manari village.

Set up tents.

Free time for bathing.

Dinner time.

Trek briefing for day 7.

Bed time.


Day 7 - All day reast at Manari village

Rise and shine.

Breakfast.

Free time all day.

Lunch for trekkers.

Afternoon tea.

Youth Choir for entertainment.

Story telling.

Dinner time.

Trek briefing for day 8.

Bed time.


Day 8 - Manari Village to Ofie Creek

Rise and shine.

Breakfast time.

Trek briefing.

Start trekking to Ofie Creek, trek time is 13 hours.

Lunch break at Naoro campsite.

Sight seeing at the gold mine site.

Arrive at Ofie Creek campsite, set up tents.

Dinner time.

Bed time.


Day 9 - Ofie Creek Campsite to Good Water Campsite

Rise and shine.

Breakfast time.

Trek briefing.

Start rekking to Good Water, trek time is 11 hours.

Break at Ioriba for 30 minutes.

Lunch at Vahure Guest house campsite.

Overnight at Good Water campsite.

Set up tents at campsite.

Free time for trekkers and opportunity for a swim.

Dinner time.

Bed time.


Day 10 - Good Water Campsite to Ower's Corner

Rise and shine.

Breakfast.

Start trekking to Ower's Corners, trek time is 4 hours and 30 minutes.

Rest at Goldie river for 1 hour.

Arrive at Ower's Corner

Hire bus will be ready for pick up and drop off at the hotel.

Gather for group photo.

Sight seeing at Bomana War Cemetary.


Day 11

We say goodbye to you all and wish you well on your journey.
