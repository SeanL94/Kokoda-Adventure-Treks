Mission Statement


KOKODA NOWAYS

      Escorting over 2500 trekkers safely across Kokoda since 2007.
      We specialize in short and long trekking | sightseeing | canoeing | rafting

Kokoda Noways is named after a famous battle during world war 2 where the
Japanese had a Battalion of Australian troops completely surrounded atop a
large waterfall along the Kokoda trail.

The large waterfall to the right is the place where it happened and also where
David's village is. Anyway the Aussie had "NOWAY" of escape and the only
way to retreat was to go down the waterfall and that is what they did and this
is where the name came from.

To us it has special meaning.
